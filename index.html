WHAT WE DO

How we help

We’ve partnered with local organizations and researched extensively to compile a comprehensive database of homeless resources in Durham. This easy-to-navigate database connects you to essential services such as shelter, food assistance, healthcare, job placement, and more. Our goal is to provide quick and direct access to the support you need, helping you find the right resources at the right time.

How does it work?

With just three simple clicks, you can easily input the resource you need—whether it's shelter, food assistance, healthcare, or job placement—and instantly receive a list of organizations and groups that can help. Our goal is to provide you with quick and direct access to the support you need when you need it.

WHAT WE OFFER

Our mission is to provide communications between those who are offering homeless and impoverished peoples resources.

ACCESSIBILITY

Ensure our resource is easy to navigate, with simple language, intuitive design, and accessibility features.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Establish credibility by providing verified and up-to-date information about resources (shelters, food banks, healthcare, etc.). Transparency in partnerships and how resources are vetted will also foster trust.

EMPOWERMENT

Offer tools and resources that encourage self-sufficiency, such as job training programs, mental health support, or housing assistance, with a focus on helping individuals regain stability and independence.
